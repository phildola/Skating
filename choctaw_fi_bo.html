Choctaw (FI → BO) Footwork

Detailed coaching card.

What it is

Choctaw from FI to BO. Inside to outside; strong check on exit.

Phases

Entry

  • Ride FI with tall posture; free leg back/low; arms compact.

Turn

  • Rise ankle→knee→hip at the top of the lobe.
  • Rotate/snap as required; feet tight; head steady.

Exit

  • Hold BO with a small check; free leg extends; continue flow.

Counts / Timing

Ride → Rise → Rotate/Snap → Check → Exit

Arm & Leg Timeline

  • Arms tiny; elbows near ribs.
  • Free foot close at rise; toe pointed on exit.
  • Head fixed on travel line.

Common Mistakes & Fixes

  • Flats at turn → keep ankle/knee roll.
  • Early shoulder swing → wait to rise.
  • Travel → reduce arm amplitude; feet tighter.

Practice Routine

Dots & Imitations

  • Mark entry/turn/exit dots; two‑foot timing x2 lengths.

Singles

  • 6 reps per side focusing on rise timing.

Linked Flow

  • Serpentine linking 3 turns with even spacing.

Off‑Ice Support

  • Pallof press 3×12
  • Ankle mobility 3×10
  • Single‑leg balance 3×30 s

Weekly Structure

  • D1 timing
  • D2 exits
  • D3 linking

Self‑Check Cues

  • Rise before rotate?
  • Exit held 2 counts?
  • No heel rock?

Progression Notes

  • Harder entries (cross‑roll/choctaw)
  • Increase speed while keeping shapes small

Improving This Element

  • Smaller shapes; steady head; micro knee pulses.
