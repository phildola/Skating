Camel Spin Spins

Detailed coaching card.

What it is

Horizontal torso line with pelvis level; long free leg; sustain rotation without travel.

Phases

Entry

  • Posture tall; arms narrow; head steady; align hips over edge.

Load/Set

  • Ankle→knee flex; feet close; shoulders stacked.

Action

  • Commit to the element with vertical rise/rotation or depth/lean as appropriate.

Exit/Check

  • Re‑stack; check shoulders; continue flow on clean edge.

Counts / Timing

Entry 1–2 • Hook 3 • Set position • Sustain • Exit/check

Arm & Leg Timeline

  • Arms: compact on approach → quick zip at take‑off/pull‑in → open/check on exit.
  • Legs: knees/feet close on load → immediate pull/tight axis → controlled extension on exit.

Common Mistakes & Fixes

  • Travel on set → center upright first; move to position in 1 count.
  • Wide arms/leg → shrink shapes; elbows brush ribs.
  • Speed loss → micro knee pulses; small shapes; forward pressure.

Practice Routine

Centering

  • Two‑foot to one‑foot centers x6; target ring size ≤ blade length.

Position

  • 3× entries to position with 2–3 rev holds; no travel.

Exits

  • 6× enter→stop→exit drills focusing on clean check.

Off‑Ice Support

  • Floor spinner 3×30 s
  • Arabesque holds 3×30 s (camel family)
  • Wall sits 3×30 s (sit family)

Weekly Structure

  • D1 center & stop travel
  • D2 position & speed
  • D3 entries/exits & variations

Self‑Check Cues

  • Center inside ring?
  • Shapes small & stacked?
  • Exit clean?

Progression Notes

  • Change edge/foot/position only after stable center
  • Increase revs without enlarging trace

Improving This Element

  • Quiet head; elbows in; pressure just forward of blade center.

Progressive Learning

Stage 1 — Foundation

  • Two‑foot→one‑foot centers on a spot; stop travel within 1 rev.
  • Upright/backspin foundation; elbows brush ribs; forward blade pressure.
  • Position primer at half speed (pelvis level for camel; stable knee for sit).
  • Off‑ice: Floor spinner 3×30 s; Arabesque holds 3×30 s (camel); Wall sits 3×30 s (sit).
  • For flying entries: hop compact; catch center within 1 rev.

Stage 2 — Integration

  • Entry→center→position in 3 counts; 2–3 revs at small shapes.
  • Anti‑travel: micro knee pulses; quiet head; elbows in.
  • Enter/exit six times, stopping travel each time.
  • Off‑ice: Thoracic mobility 3×30 s; Anti‑extension core 3×12.

Stage 3 — Mastery

  • +1 rev vs baseline without extra travel.
  • Add variations (edge/foot/position) only if center stays inside ring.
  • Polish: choreo arms within cone of balance.
  • Off‑ice: Metronome spins 3×30 s; Hamstring/glute strength 3×12.
