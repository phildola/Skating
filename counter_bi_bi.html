Counter (BI → BI) Footwork

Detailed coaching card.

What it is

Against‑curve turn; crisp snap and firm check; spacing consistent.

Phases

Entry

  • Posture tall; arms narrow; head steady; align hips over edge.

Load/Set

  • Ankle→knee flex; feet close; shoulders stacked.

Action

  • Commit to the element with vertical rise/rotation or depth/lean as appropriate.

Exit/Check

  • Re‑stack; check shoulders; continue flow on clean edge.

Counts / Timing

Ride → Rise → Rotate/Snap → Check → Exit (use metronome 92–108 BPM after shape is stable)

Arm & Leg Timeline

  • Arms: compact on approach → quick zip at take‑off/pull‑in → open/check on exit.
  • Legs: knees/feet close on load → immediate pull/tight axis → controlled extension on exit.

Common Mistakes & Fixes

  • Flats at turn → maintain ankle/knee roll through rise.
  • Early shoulder swing → wait till rise.
  • Heel rock → keep pressure slightly forward; quiet head.

Practice Routine

Dots & Imitations

  • Mark entry/turn/exit dots; two‑foot timing x2 lengths.

Singles

  • 6 reps per side focusing on rise timing and checks.

Linked Flow

  • Serpentine/sequence linking turns with even spacing.

Off‑Ice Support

  • Pallof press 3×12/side
  • Ankle mobility 3×10
  • Single‑leg balance 3×30 s

Weekly Structure

  • D1 timing
  • D2 exits
  • D3 linking

Self‑Check Cues

  • Rise before rotate?
  • Exit held 2 counts?
  • No heel rock?

Progression Notes

  • Harder entries (cross‑roll/choctaw)
  • Increase tempo while keeping small shapes

Improving This Element

  • Smaller shapes; steady head; micro knee pulses.

Progressive Learning

Stage 1 — Foundation

  • Two‑foot imitations on dots for entry/turn/exit; say counts aloud.
  • Edge purity: clear FO/FI/BO/BI with knee/ankle roll; avoid flats.
  • Off‑ice: Rope 3×2 min; Ankle rockers 3×10; Pallof 3×12/side.

Stage 2 — Integration

  • Single‑foot turns on marked lobes; rise at top then snap; feet tight.
  • Equal lobe spacing with cones; film one length for consistency.
  • Off‑ice: Anti‑rotation band presses 3×12/side; Balance board 3×30 s.
  • Use metronome 92–108 BPM once shape is stable.

Stage 3 — Mastery

  • Link the full pattern at slow tempo; then increase speed keeping shapes small.
  • Quality checks: no heel rock; quiet head; clean checks on exits.
  • Off‑ice: Lateral bounds 3×10/side; Rope 3×2 min.
